About Counsel

A practice built on precision and quiet trust

Julient Tuhaise was called to the Uganda Bar in 2011 and has since built a chambers known less for noise and more for outcomes. Her work spans contested land disputes in Wakiso, cross-border corporate structuring, and the kind of family matters that never make it to a headline but change a household for good.

She approaches every file the same way: understand the human situation before the legal one. That order rarely changes the strategy, but it changes how a client experiences the process.
