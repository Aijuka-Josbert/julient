# Juliet Tuhaise Advocates – Legal Counsel Website

A modern, responsive, and accessible legal counsel website for **Juliet Tuhaise**, an Advocate and Senior Counsel based in Kampala, Uganda.

---

## 🚀 Live Demo

[View the live site →](https://tuhaisejuliet.com)

---

## 🛠️ Built With

- **HTML5** – Semantic markup
- **CSS3** – Custom properties, flexbox, grid, animations
- **JavaScript (Vanilla)** – Scroll reveals, dynamic content, form validation, theme toggle
- **EmailJS** – Contact form email delivery (no backend required)
- **GitHub Pages** – Free hosting

---

## ✨ Features

- ✅ Fully responsive (desktop, tablet, mobile)
- ✅ Dark / Light theme toggle (persisted via localStorage)
- ✅ Scroll-triggered animations (IntersectionObserver)
- ✅ Interactive timeline (career milestones)
- ✅ Gallery with filter and lightbox
- ✅ FAQ accordion
- ✅ Dynamic practice area detail pages (10 areas, single template)
- ✅ Contact form with client-side validation + EmailJS integration
- ✅ Custom cursor (desktop only)
- ✅ Preloader
- ✅ Cookie consent banner
- ✅ SEO meta tags + Open Graph
- ✅ robots.txt & sitemap.xml for search engines
- ✅ Privacy Policy & Terms of Service (single `legal.html` page)
